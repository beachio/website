Frequently Asked Questions

What Does a Beach Apprenticeship Cost?

It is my goal that a Beach Apprenticeship will cost successful graduates nothing. That is, by the end of the 12 month programme, the cost of the education for one apprentice is completely covered and absorbed by the programme. Find Out How

Where is it based?

The Beach Apprenticeship programme is for the modern age. As such, it is not restricted to any particular location or cubicle. A massive part of what you learn is the maturity to decide the best ways for you to be most productive in learning and building. Knowing when to collaborate, when to focus. We make that process easy, by partnering with the newest co-working spaces in Dorset to provide FREE access for our Apprentices. We come together regularly for team sessions, mentoring, office hours, presentations, code reviews.

When Can I Start?

We are now in the process of opening up applications for our first batch, starting early in 2015. You can apply now to be part of a new breed of builders, people who can really make a change in this world.

How is your curriculum created?

More than the curriculum, it is our focus on Mentor driven learning that we really care about. Mentors who are experts in their field, based all around the world, are on hand to guide you through the course, help when you get stuck, dive deeper into subjects and unlock new opportunities. Our world class curriculum features the best of the worldwide online Developer training resources, from the super high value Bloc.io, to the entertaining and accessible Treehouse, to the popular and fun Codeschool and even the free and widely available Codecademy.
